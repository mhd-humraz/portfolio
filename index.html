About Me

I’m Humraz, a BCA student at MES M.K. Mackar Pillay College,passionate about technology, creativity, and innovation. I'm an event planner for the Computer Science Department Association BYTE BEGIN, where I help organize tech fests, tournaments, and workshops. I love blending tech with creativity—whether it's through graphic design, developing educational websites, or integrating AI assistants.
I run a small business called Origin Media, where I create and sell custom wall posters, and I’m also working on a website for it. Besides that, I’m building a platform to teach programming languages like HTML and C in a clear and engaging way, with plans to include AI help for learners.
My background includes being an SPC Commander, designing posters using Canva, exploring WordPress, and planning to dive into backend development soon. I also enjoy drawing, learning guitar, solving tough problems, and working with a close-knit team of amazing friends and teammates.
